Rules:

There are 42 questions in total. 6 questions about 7 doctors.

From the 1st Doctor William Hartnell to the 7th Doctor Sylvester McCoy.

The TV movie is the dividing line between classic Who and regenerated Who and is therefore not included in this quiz.

When you press START a question will be displayed and the 30 second countdown will begin.

Each question has 3 possible answers. Only one answer is correct.

You can simplify the quiz by selecting SIMPLIFY to remove one of the incorrect answers.

You score 2 points for a correct answer. Unless you simplified in which case you only score 1 point for a correct answer.

Each time a question is displayed the 30 second countdown resets and begins again.

The game ends when you answer incorrectly or the timer runs down to zero.
