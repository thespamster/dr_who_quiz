Rules:

There are 42 questions in total. 6 questions about 7 doctors.

From the 1st Doctor back in the 60's to the 7th Doctor in the 80's.

The TV movie is the dividing line between classic Who and regenerated Who and is therefore not included in this quiz.

When you press START a question will be displayed and the 30 second countdown will begin.

Each question has 3 possible answers. Only one answer is correct.

You can make the question easier to answer by selecting EASY to remove one of the incorrect answers.

Get the answer correct and the answer button will turn green. Get it wrong and it will turn red and the correct button will turn green.

You score 2 points for a correct answer. Unless you selected EASY in which case a correct answer scores 1 point.

Each time a question is displayed the 30 second countdown resets and begins again.

The game ends when you answer incorrectly or the timer reaches zero.
